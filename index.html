Mayer Multiple

Another technical indicator we’re watching that makes us think we’ve hit the bottom is called the Mayer Multiple. The MM measures the difference between an asset’s price and its 200-day MA. It essentially quantifies how far a certain asset has diverged from its long-term price trend.
